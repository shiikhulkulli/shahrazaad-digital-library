About ShahraZaad Digital Library

ShahraZaad Digital Library is an online platform dedicated to providing open and easy access to a wide range of e-books, educational resources, and digital content for learners, readers, and curious minds of all ages.

ShahraZaad was created by a Somali book lover named Saacid Shiikh Yuusuf, with a passion for promoting literacy and knowledge through open access to books. The name "ShahraZaad" is inspired by Shahrazad, the beloved fictional storyteller who wove the enchanting tales of One Thousand and One Nights story {ألف ليلة وليلة}. Just as Shahrazad connected stories to keep imaginations alive, this library connects readers to knowledge that lasts a lifetime.

Our mission is to promote knowledge, literacy, and learning by offering a user-friendly library where books can be read online or downloaded for offline use — all at no cost. Whether you’re a student, a teacher, or simply a book lover, ShahraZaad has something for you.

What We Offer

  • A curated collection of educational and general interest e-books.
  • Instant online reading .
  • Free download options for personal use.
  • Mobile-friendly and accessible design.

Our Vision

We believe that education should be free, accessible, and open to everyone, regardless of background. ShahraZaad Digital Library was created to support this vision by bringing books into the hands of those who need them most.
